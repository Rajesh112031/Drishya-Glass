About Us

Welcome to DRISHYA Where Glass meets Art

At Drishya Glass Art Emporium , we bring together the timeless beauty of glass artistry and fine craftsmanship. Our emporium is a celebration of creativity , where hand-blown glass ,intricates sculptures , and stunning paintings come to life , reflecting a perfect blend of tradition and modern innovation.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for art and glassmaking ,Drishya was born out of a vision to create a space where artistry is cherished , and every piece tells a story. Our Journey began with a deep admiration for glassblowers ,painters ,and Sculptors who transform raw materials into breathtaking masterpieces . Today ,we stand as hub for art collectors ,enthusiasts ,and creators who share our love for exquisite craftmanship.

Why Choose Drishya ?

Custom Creations Available - Bring your vision to life with personalized designs.
